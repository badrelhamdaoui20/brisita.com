    Frequently Asked Questions

    WHEN WILL MY ITEMS SHIP

    All orders are subject to a handling period before shipment, usually within 1 to 3 business days.

    Most orders leave the warehouse immediately after the items have successfully processed .

    You will then receive notification of both processing and shipment. In many instances, we are shipping from our warehouses in the United state. For ALL orders outside United state, the order could take about 5 weeks to be deliverd!

    Orders may be canceled if they have not been processed and shipped. After they have been processed they may not be canceled.

    Please make sure to provide the correct shipping address. BRISITA.COM can not be responsible for any additional shipping fees incurred to ship to the correct address. 

    WHEN ARE MY ITEMS ARRIVE?

    For specialty and promotional items, please allow up to 5 to 20 business day delivery time as they may be shipping from other countries

    WHERE ARE MY ITEMS COMMING FROM?

    Our primary warehouse is in th United state and majority of our product will be shipped from there. 

    WILL MY ITEMS BE SENT IN ONE PAKAGE?

    For logistical reasons, items will sometimes be sent in separate packages. please keep this in mind when receiving deliveries. if you have any other questions, please Contact us

    DO YOU SHIP OUTSIDE THE UNITED STATE

    We wurrently do NOT ship the united state

    HOW DO YOU DETERMINE YOUR "COMPARD TO" PRICES?

    We get these prices from our wholesalers or for competitor analysis.

    DO YOU SELL WHOLESALE?

    We do sell in wholesale for certain sellers. Email us for details: contact@brisita.com.

    WHERE ARE RETURNS SENT?

    Returns can be processed only after the return request is verfied with our support team. Please E-mail us at contact@brisita.com or SMS at +1(206) 848-5740 for the returns address.
